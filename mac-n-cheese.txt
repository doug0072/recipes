Ingredients:
 - 1/2 stick butter
 - 2 cups milk
 - 1 lb sharp cheddar cheese
 - 4 tablespoons of flour
 - 1 lb macaroni 

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter and stir in flour until starts to foam.
  2. Add milk and stir until creamy.
  3. Add cheese and stir until melted.
  4. Combine with cooked pasta.
  
 Optional Upgrades:
  - Broccoli
  - Spinach
  - Diced tomatoes
  - Diced bacon 
  - Ground pork
  - Garlic
  - Chilli Pepper/ Cayenne Pepper
